WHO WE REPRESENT

Dynamic scouting agency (VSA) was founded in june 2024 with Victor,Ruth destiny and Diana as co founders. DSA is a licensed football agency. with its base in Germany and UK. We focus on talent scouting and guiding players to achieve their full potential and ambitions on their way to a professional career. We use our wealth of solid experience, professional insight and in-depth knowledge of football to achieve this. At DSA, we are very passionate about empowering women also in the football environment. Our mission is to provide the best representation, guidance, and support for both male and female players of all ages and skill levels. We believe in values which every single player is creating and providing to their Clubs. Contact us to learn more about how we can help you achieve your goals in the world of football.
