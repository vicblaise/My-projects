ABOUT US...

Dynamic scouting agency is a licensed agency for sports management, player consulting and player placement registered DSA, which was founded in 2012 and is owned by Blaise & his wife. From its base in Germany and the UK, we takes care of national and international football players and transfers. The focus of our agency is scouting, match analysis and player advice. We We use our extensive network, many years of experience, professional insights and in-depth knowledge of football. We are passionate about promoting women football also. Our goal is to represent, advise and support players of all ages and levels in the best possible way and to bring the right players together with the right clubs for them. We always do what is best for our players, for their progress and their interests Our team consists of 2 permanent employees, Destopy and lady Diana. In addition, we work with selected and experienced scouts, licensed international football coaches and a team doctor at the highest international level. We have a deep understanding of the football industry and the unique challenges that players face. We work closely with each player to help them find the right club, negotiate contracts and develop their careers. Our ultimate goal is to help our players reach their full potential and achieve their dreams in football. As well as representing players, we also offer a range of services to football clubs. We support clubs with scouting, recruitment and contract negotiations to help them build successful teams. We are proud and we are committed to making a positive impact on the industry. Whether you are a player looking to take your game to the next level or a club looking to build a successful team, we are here for you. Contact us to find out more about how we can help you achieve your goals in the world of football.
